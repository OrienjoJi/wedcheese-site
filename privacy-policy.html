Privacy Policy

Your data should be handled with care.

This Privacy Policy explains how Wedcheese, a service provided by Orienjo Ltd. (“we,” “us,” or “our”), collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our website and mobile app.

Effective date: April 7, 2026 Contact: admin@orienjo.com

1. Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account details such as your name, email address, sign-in provider, and profile information.
  • Wedding planning data you choose to add, including budgets, checklists, guest details, notes, and preferences.
  • Usage information such as app interactions, diagnostics, crash reports, and device metadata.
  • Communications you send to us through support or feedback channels.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to operate, improve, and support Wedcheese, including to:

  • Provide the core planning features of the app.
  • Personalize recommendations, reminders, and AI-assisted experiences.
  • Maintain security, investigate abuse, and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • Respond to questions, support requests, and account-related inquiries.

3. When we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers that help us host, secure, analyze, or operate the service, and where required by law or to protect users and our platform.

4. Third-party services

Wedcheese may rely on third-party tools such as authentication, analytics, hosting, crash reporting, payments, or cloud infrastructure providers. These providers process information under their own terms and privacy commitments.

5. Data retention

We keep personal information only for as long as it is reasonably needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When you request deletion, we aim to remove or anonymize associated personal data unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or billing reasons.

6. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location (including under the UK GDPR), you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your account data.
  • Request a copy of certain personal information in a portable format.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with a local supervisory authority (such as the ICO in the UK) if you believe your privacy rights have been violated.
You can review our public deletion instructions at our Data Deletion page.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s privacy

Wedcheese is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can investigate and take appropriate action.

9. Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date on this page. Material updates may also be communicated in-app or by other reasonable means.
