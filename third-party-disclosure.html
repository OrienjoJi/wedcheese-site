Third-Party Disclosure

Services we use to power Wedcheese.

Effective date: April 7, 2026 Contact: admin@orienjo.com

1. Infrastructure & Data

To provide a seamless experience, Wedcheese relies on trusted third-party infrastructure. We use Google Cloud and Firebase (a Google service) for secure database hosting, user authentication, and crash reporting. Your data is stored securely on their servers in accordance with their strict privacy standards.

2. Artificial Intelligence

Our core AI features are powered by leading language models (such as Google Gemini/OpenAI). When you ask the AI a question or request a generated theme, your specific prompt text is securely processed by these providers to generate your answer. We do not use your personal planning data to train public AI models.

3. App Stores & Billing

All payments, subscriptions, and financial transactions are securely handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). Orienjo Ltd. does not collect, process, or store your credit card information.
