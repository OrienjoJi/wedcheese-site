Frequently asked questions

Is WedCheese free to use?

Yes. WedCheese is free to download and use. You can start planning with checklists, budgets, guests, vendors, decor, timelines, and limited daily AI help. Premium unlocks more AI capacity for active planning weeks.

What does the AI wedding planner actually do?

The AI helps with practical planning tasks such as building checklists, suggesting budget changes, drafting vendor messages, creating RSVP wording, helping with guest-list dilemmas, planning decor, and building wedding-day timelines. Where the app supports updates, you review suggested changes before applying them.

Can WedCheese replace a human wedding planner?

WedCheese is designed to help with wedding planning admin, organisation, budgeting, guests, RSVPs, decor, vendors, and timelines. It can reduce planning stress, but it does not replace the personal judgement, negotiation, emotional support, or on-the-day coordination of a professional planner.

Can guests RSVP without downloading the app?

Yes. WedCheese supports digital RSVP links so guests can reply without needing to download the app.

Can I use WedCheese for a short engagement?

Yes. WedCheese can help organise faster planning timelines by focusing on what matters first when your wedding date is close.

Is WedCheese available on iPhone and Android?

Yes. WedCheese is available on iOS and Android. Use the App Store or Google Play buttons to download the app.
