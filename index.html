Meet your AI wedding planner

Not a chatbot. A wedding planning agent that takes action.

Wedcheese's AI doesn't just answer questions — it reads your wedding profile, budget, guest list, and vendor team, then makes real changes you review and approve in one tap.

Builds your first plan from scratch

Tell it your date, budget, and style — it generates a personalized checklist, budget breakdown, and decor direction so you know exactly where to start.

Updates checklists, budget, guests, decor, and timeline — all at once

Say "add 20 guests and increase catering budget" and it proposes the guest additions, budget adjustment, and new tasks in a single structured plan you approve.

Searches vendors by location and ranks them for your style

Ask "find a photographer near Manchester" and it searches real listings, ranks them by distance and match to your wedding vibe, and lets you save favorites.

Drafts vendor inquiry and reply emails for you

It knows which vendors you've saved, their category, and booking status — then writes category-specific emails (venue availability, catering menus, photographer portfolios) ready to send.

Builds your wedding-day timeline event by event

Ask "create a schedule for my day" and it generates a ceremony-to-last-dance run sheet you can edit, reorder, and share with your wedding party.

Helps with the emotional side too

Write your vows together, draft thank-you notes, handle guest-list dilemmas diplomatically, and get a calming pep talk when wedding stress hits.

The calm way to plan your wedding

Beyond AI, Wedcheese gives you structured tools for every part of planning — guests, decor, vendors, budget, and timelines — all connected and always in sync.

RSVPs and guest details without spreadsheet chaos

Keep track of responses, plus-ones, meal choices, and guest notes in one place so you always know who is coming and what they need.

Decor & Style tied to your real checklist

Plan decor by area—ceremony, tables, signage, personal touches—and whether your venue supplies most of it, you mix in DIY, or you source it yourself. Track items and costs, sync what you commit with your Decor & Style budget, and use your AI assistant for ideas, vendor-ready wording, and budget-aware suggestions.

Saved vendors from AI search—and your own list

Save vendors you find through Wedcheese’s AI search or add your own, then keep type, booking status, and notes in one place. The same Saved hub holds your wedding schedule and a notebook for ideas, so contacts and next steps don’t scatter across apps.

Budget clarity without losing the joy

Stay grounded in your numbers, understand priorities, and make thoughtful trade-offs while keeping the celebration you really want in view.

Checklists and timelines that keep both of you aligned

Turn big wedding ideas into manageable next steps with clear milestones, reminders, and planning progress you can follow together.
