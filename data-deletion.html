Data Deletion

How to delete your Wedcheese account and data.

We want it to be straightforward for users to remove their information. This page explains the steps available for deleting a Wedcheese account and related data, in compliance with App Store and Google Play guidelines.

Effective date: April 7, 2026 Contact: admin@orienjo.com

1. Delete directly inside the app

If you can access your account, the fastest and most secure way to request deletion is directly from inside Wedcheese:

  • Open the Wedcheese app.
  • Navigate to the Me (Profile) tab.
  • Go to your Account Settings.
  • Select the Delete Account option.
  • Confirm the request to permanently remove your account and data.

2. What is deleted

Your deletion request is intended to permanently remove or anonymize personal data associated with your account, including:

  • Profile and sign-in information stored by Wedcheese.
  • Wedding planning content such as notes, preferences, budgets, guest lists, and task data.
  • Associated application records that are no longer required for security or legal compliance.

3. What may be retained for a limited period

Some limited information may be retained where strictly necessary for fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, legal obligations, or app store billing records. This may include secure backup data that is automatically deleted on a rolling basis.

4. If you cannot access the app (Web Deletion Request)

If you have uninstalled the app or are unable to sign in, you can request account deletion via email. Please email admin@orienjo.com using the exact email address associated with your Wedcheese account and include “Account Deletion Request” in the subject line.

To help us process your request safely and verify your identity, please include the platform you used to create the account (e.g., Apple, Google, or Email).

5. Subscriptions and purchases

Important: Deleting your Wedcheese account does not automatically cancel active subscriptions purchased through Apple or Google. Those subscriptions must be managed and canceled directly through your Apple App Store or Google Play Store account settings.

If you have questions about account deletion, data retention, or your privacy rights, please contact us:
