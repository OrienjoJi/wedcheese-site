Organize Client Tasks Without Spreadsheets

Freelance consultants juggle multiple clients, each with their own set of deliverables and deadlines. TaskPilot solves the core problem: keeping every client task in one place without spreadsheets. Instead of toggling between email, Excel files, and sticky notes, you get a dedicated workspace where tasks live alongside client context.

The platform captures tasks directly from your inbox, eliminating manual data entry. When a client emails a request, TaskPilot extracts it and places it in the right client folder automatically. This means fewer missed deliverables and less time spent hunting for where you wrote down that urgent revision request.

Weekly Deadline Visibility

Knowing what's due this week is critical for prioritization. TaskPilot displays upcoming deadlines in a clear view, so you can see at a glance which clients need attention soonest. This weekly snapshot helps you allocate hours efficiently and avoid the last-minute scramble that often comes from scattered task lists.

Grouping tasks by client makes it simple to understand workload distribution. You can see if one client's requests are dominating your schedule or if deadlines are spread evenly across your portfolio. This visibility supports better capacity planning and helps you decide when to push back on timelines or adjust rates.

Simplified Task Management

The goal of TaskPilot is straightforward: reduce friction in how you track work. No complex workflows, no unnecessary features. When tasks live in one organized system rather than across email folders and notes, context switching disappears. You spend less time managing your task list and more time on billable work.

For consultants billing by the hour or project, accurate task tracking directly impacts invoicing accuracy. TaskPilot's centralized approach makes it easier to recall which deliverables you completed for each client and when, supporting transparent client communication and professional billing practices.
