What Is WedCheese?

WedCheese is an AI-powered wedding planner app available on iOS and Android. It combines intelligent automation with practical tools to help couples organize every detail of their big day. The app centralizes your timeline, budget, guests, decor ideas, and vendor information in one place, then uses an AI assistant trained on your specific wedding data to guide you through the planning process.

Your wedding plan finally has a brain. Instead of juggling spreadsheets, notes, and vendor emails, WedCheese keeps everything connected and intelligent.

Core Features That Simplify Planning

WedCheese offers a comprehensive toolkit designed to ditch the wedding planning overwhelm. The checklist timeline includes accelerated phases for short engagements, so whether you're planning with an extended timeframe or a compressed schedule, the app adjusts your task flow accordingly.

The budget tracker provides real-time category tracking, deposit monitoring, and balance visibility. You always know where your money stands without manual calculations.

Guest management is friction-free: create a guest list and generate shareable RSVP links that work without requiring guests to download the app. They can respond directly from a simple web link.

The decor planner lets you explore color palettes and budget-aware styling ideas, helping you visualize your aesthetic while respecting your financial boundaries.

Your vendor binder stores vendor profiles, booking status, and notes in one searchable location. No more hunting through email threads or lost business cards.

How the AI Assistant Works

WedCheese includes an AI assistant grounded in your couple's real wedding data. Because it understands your timeline, budget, guest count, and design preferences, it provides suggestions tailored to your actual plan, not generic advice. Ask it questions, get recommendations, or let it flag tasks coming due based on your vendor deadlines and engagement length.

Getting Started

Download WedCheese free on iOS and Android. Your account includes 5 daily AI credits at no cost. If you need additional AI capacity, a premium subscription is available to extend your access beyond the daily limit.

The app works across devices, so your wedding data stays synchronized whether you're planning on your phone or tablet.

Why Couples Choose WedCheese

Traditional wedding planning spreads your information across multiple tools: a notes app, a spreadsheet, a Pinterest board, vendor emails, and your phone's calendar. WedCheese unifies these into one intelligent system.

The AI learns your preferences and constraints, then helps you make faster, better-informed decisions. Accelerated timelines mean short-engagement couples don't feel pressured by one-size-fits-all schedules. Shareable RSVP links eliminate barriers for guests, boosting response rates without friction.

When every detail is connected, planning becomes less chaotic. WedCheese transforms your wedding from a collection of disconnected tasks into a coherent, manageable plan.
