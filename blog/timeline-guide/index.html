How to Build a Stress-Free Wedding Timeline (Even in 6 Months)

Published June 2026~6 min read

The moment you announce your engagement, the clock starts ticking. Suddenly, everyone is asking you about dates, venues, and colour palettes. If you are starting from a blank slate, the sheer volume of tasks can feel completely paralysing.

Whether you have a luxurious two-year engagement or a fast-tracked six-month sprint, the secret to preserving your sanity is a bulletproof wedding timeline.

Why spreadsheets fail you

Most couples start by downloading a generic wedding checklist from Google. The problem? Spreadsheets are static. They don't adjust if you book your venue a month late, and they certainly don't tell you how to negotiate with a caterer once you find one.

You end up staring at a massive list of 150 unchecked boxes, feeling more anxious than when you started.
