Support

Need help with Wedcheese?

If you have a question about your account, subscription, planning tools, or app experience, contact the Wedcheese support team by email.

Email: admin@orienjo.com

Contact support

Email us at admin@orienjo.com and our team will get back to you as soon as possible.

For faster help, please include the email address used for your Wedcheese account, your device type, and a short description of the issue.

Email Support

Useful details to include

  • The email address connected to your Wedcheese account.
  • Your device type, such as iPhone, Android, or web browser.
  • What you were trying to do when the issue happened.
  • Any error message or screenshot that can help us understand the problem.

In-app support

If you are using the Wedcheese mobile app, you can also go to Me > Help & Support to send a support request directly from inside the app.
